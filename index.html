Know your brew

Understanding various coffee brewing methods is like holding the key to a treasure chest. Each method, from the Chemex's clean profiles to the French Press's robust depths, allows you to tailor your coffee to your mood and needs. With the precision of an Espresso, you can kick-start a sluggish morning, or with the gentle touch of a V60, ease into a peaceful afternoon. You'll never be stuck with a dull cup again!

Chemex

Chemex is for the purist at heart. Its unique design and brewing technique produce a coffee that is clean, pure and free of bitterness, emphasizing the subtle nuances of the coffee bean. If you delight in the clarity and complexity of flavors and value a method that doubles as a centerpiece, the Chemex is your perfect match. Ideal for light to medium roast beans.
